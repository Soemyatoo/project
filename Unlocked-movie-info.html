Unlocked
Unlocked tells us the story of a woman called Na Mi. She is drunk one day and loses her phone on the bus. A man called Jun Yeong picks up her phone and informs Na Mi about the incident the next day. After this encounter, a series of horrific incidents follow Na Mi both in her personal life and in her professional life.
